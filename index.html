Explore the Amazing World of Mangroves!
Mangroves are unique ecosystems that provide many benefits to humans and wildlife. But where can you find them and what makes them so special?

Biodiversity Hotspots

Mangrove forests can be found on the saltwater coasts of 118 tropical and subtropical countries.

Climate Mitigation

As carbon-capturing powerhouses, mangroves play a crucial role in mitigating climate change.

Natural Barriers

Mangroves act as natural shields, reducing the impact of coastal erosion and shielding communities from the wrath of storms.
